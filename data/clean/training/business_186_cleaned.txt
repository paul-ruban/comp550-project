huge rush for jet airways shares indian airline jet airways ' initial public offering was oversubscribed 16.2 times , bankers said on friday . over 85 % of the bids were at the higher end of the price range of 1,050-1,125 rupees ( $ 24- $ 26 ) . jet airways , a low-fare airline , was founded by london-based ex-travel agent naresh goya , and controls 45 % of the indian domestic airline market . it sold 20 % of its equity or 17.2 million shares in a bid to raise up to $ 443m ( â£230.8m ) . the price at which its shares will begin trading will be agreed over the weekend , bankers said . `` the demand for the ipo was impressive . we believe that over the next two years , the domestic aviation sector promises strong growth , even though fuel prices could be high , '' said hiten mehta , manager of merchant banking firm , fortune financial services . india began to open up its domestic airline market - previously dominated by state-run carrier indian airlines - in the 1990s . jet began flying in 1993 and now has competitors including air deccan and air sahara . budget carriers kingfisher airlines and spicejet are planning to launch operations in may this year . jet has 42 aircraft and runs 271 scheduled flights daily within india . it recently won government permission to fly to london , singapore and kuala lumpur .
